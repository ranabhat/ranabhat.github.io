About Me

I'm from Pokhara, Nepal and moved to Finland to pursue my Bachelor's of Engineering in Information Technology. During my studies, I built an embedded project—an Arduino and Python-based solution enabling students to learn signal processing from home. This sparked my passion for Python and embedded systems on Raspberry Pi.

My first role at Unified Chargers Oy, a Finnish startup building 30kW DC charging stations, introduced me to IoT and EV charging infrastructure. That experience opened doors to the EV domain, where I've spent years on fleet automation and management.

Today, I work at Evenli, a Helsinki-based EV charging solutions company. I'm involved in charger provisioning, SDLC workflows, building internal tools for ops automation, and delivering business-critical features from the charger perspective. I apply my CLI-first workflow and leverage AI agents to solve infrastructure challenges at scale.
